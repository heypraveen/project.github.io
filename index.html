ABOUT

Established in the year 2003, we, Addison Industries, have become one of the manufacturers, exporters of an extensive range of Industrial Reactors, Storage Tanks, Nutsche Filters, Fractionating Columns etc. In addition, we also provide a wide variety of Multiple Effect Evaporators. These are demanded widely for distinguishing features that comprise long functional life, high efficiency, optimum performance and robust construction. Owing to these, our range is serving the mixing, heat transfer, evaporation, filtration, drying and storage needs of the chemical and bulk drug industry.

Being an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization, we believe in offering quality range of industrial processing equipment. Our organization is backed by well-established manufacturing unit, which is equipped with sophisticated machines and tools. The entire unit is managed by a team of professionals, which has profound knowledge and rich experience in the respective field. We have carved a niche in the industry due to our unique manufacturing process systems that enable us to maintain the strong customer base. Some of our reputed clients include Divis Laboratories, Mylan Laboratories, Albattal group - Saudi, i3 Technologies -US, Zydus Cadila, MSN Labs, Rachem Pharma, HBL, Neuland labs, SNF, DRDL etc.
